# brand/ — Brand Assets & Copy Guidelines

Inherits all brand-wide truths from the root CLAUDE.md. The voice section there
is canonical; this file holds visual identity and the specific copy decisions
made to date.

## Voice (see root CLAUDE.md for the full rules)
Observational. Spare. Direct. State facts, leave space. The community fills in
meaning. This is non-negotiable and applies everywhere.

## Name & domain
- Brand: **+Prayer**
- Domain: **plusprayer.com**, displayed lowercase (sits quietly; consistent with
  the spare voice — CamelCase performs, lowercase states). Lowercase also avoids
  the "P"+"P" visual bump in "PlusPrayer".

## Color
- Base navy: `#0C1B29`
- Accent electric blue: `#0580F9`
- Deliberately distinct from competitor Hallow's purple.
- (Website hero has used a `#EDF0F5` background with a watercolor world-map
  texture filling praying hands.)

## Logo
Organic cluster of circles with a "+" as a white negative-space cutout.

## Taglines & lines (decided)
- **Web tagline (current/preferred):** "One list. Everyone. Every day."
  - "Everyone" carries reciprocity, scale, and inclusion in one word, with no
    tradition implied. Rejected variants used "faith" or "prayer" as nouns
    (too religiously specific).
- **App-store tagline (older draft):** "One list. Every faith. Every day."
  → reconcile to the "Everyone" version during app development.
- **Splash line:** "Your name, carried by the world."
- **Home headline:** "Your name, carried by the world."
- **Home subhead:** "A daily prayer list. Real names, real intentions."

## Positioning
Tradition-neutral, interfaith, non-denominational. Founder story rooted in
universal human goodwill rather than any specific faith tradition — treated as an
honest, defensible basis for the interfaith angle. The brand does not perform
community or spirituality.

## Monetization stance (brand-facing)
No brand deals, no donations. The app (freemium subscription) is the sole
meaningful revenue vehicle. Brand integrity is protected ahead of monetization.
The honest one-sentence description of the model: free members are always carried
on the list; paid members can carry additional loved ones. That sentence should
always sound generous, because it is.

## Website
Framer (+ Kit for email). The website directs traffic OUTWARD to social, where
the engagement flywheel lives. The site is a supporting player, not the product.
Pages: home, about, list/pray, faq, app (placeholder + email capture).
