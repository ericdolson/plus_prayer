Heavenly Father,

With humble hearts, we lift up in prayer the 2,345 souls on our +Prayer Community Prayer List. You know each name, each story, each heart. We ask You to surround them with Your mercy, Your healing, and Your peace. May their burdens be lightened, their spirits strengthened, and their hope renewed.

Lord, pour out Your grace upon them — those seeking comfort, those asking for healing, and those yearning for closeness with You. May the intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary and all the saints accompany their intentions.

And Father, we give You thanks.
Thank You for this sacred space of shared prayer.
Thank You for the unseen ways You are working even now.
Thank You for drawing us closer to You and to one another through the gift of faith.

In Jesus' name we pray,
Amen.
